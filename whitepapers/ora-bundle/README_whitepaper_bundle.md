# ORA White Papers Index

Global public index for the ORA white paper bundle.

## Bundle overview

This bundle currently groups two complementary publications:

- `ORA_CORE_OS_ORA_RAG_MODULAR_ARCHITECTURE_WhitePaper.md`
  Main strategic white paper in French on ORA Core OS modular architecture, need compilation, governance and the place of ORA RAG in the system.
- `ORA_CORE_RAG_ARCHITECTURE_WhitePaper.md`
  Shorter technical white paper focused on ORA RAG as a governed retrieval architecture.

## Recommended reading order

1. Read `ORA_CORE_OS_ORA_RAG_MODULAR_ARCHITECTURE_WhitePaper.md` for the system-level view.
2. Read `ORA_CORE_RAG_ARCHITECTURE_WhitePaper.md` for the retrieval-layer deep dive.

## Coverage map

The bundle covers:

- the canonical modular architecture of the agent layer
- the role of natural-language need compilation
- the distinction between mandatory core modules, stable capability modules and optional identity layers
- the architectural role of `ORA RAG` as a governed retrieval layer
- the separation between canon, client corpora and experimental documentary routes

## Editorial logic

The editorial intent is to make each paper readable on its own while preserving a clear common entrypoint.
This index acts as that entrypoint and as the navigation hub for the bundle.

## Public note

This publication focuses on architecture, governance, modularity and documentary integrity.
It excludes private delivery packs, client-specific onboarding material and non-public operational instructions.
